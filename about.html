About Me

A backend developer still early in the story, already building things people use.

I'm currently a part-time backend developer at Code Café, where I lead backend work on a live production system for a manufacturing client and contribute full-stack to a client proof-of-concept. The role grew directly out of a one-week internship at Agrisol's IT department: what started as a week of learning turned into a full-time placement, and then into an ongoing part-time role with the same manager, now running his own software venture.

Databases and APIs are where I feel most at home: designing a schema that actually holds up, then building the service and API layer on top of it. I've also picked up a fair amount of automation and AI-driven workflow experience along the way, building n8n pipelines, integrating AI-driven data extraction, and using tools like Claude to work faster without cutting corners. Outside of paid work, I've led student teams through two university projects, mentored first-year students as a lab assistant, and I'm working toward finishing my BSc in November 2026.

I'm looking to specialise further in backend development, while staying open to full-stack work as opportunities come up.
